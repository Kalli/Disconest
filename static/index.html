Vinyl sounds better, looks better, feels better and even smells better. But digital does have its benefits, musical metadata is one of them. Having the key, tempo and other data for your records would be useful! Disconest uses the The Echonest and Discogs apis to find this information about records and cds registered on Discogs.

Enter a Discogs url in the box above to try it out or drag this Disconest! bookmarklet to your bookmarks bar and click it when you are on a Discogs release page.

If you want to use Disconest to get this information for your entire Discogs collection you can use the command line version.
